# 🦍 FLASH LITE RUM HAM 🦍

## THE MEAT
- 2 thick ham steaks (Fast meat)
- Dark rum (Small bottle)

## THE GLAZE
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- Dash of cinnamon

## THE WORK
1. **Pan fire:** Heat heavy pan on medium heat.
2. **Sear:** Brown ham steaks 3 minutes each side.
3. **Booze:** Pour 1/4 cup rum into pan. Careful of fire breath (flame).
4. **Glaze:** Mix sugar, honey, mustard, cinnamon in small cup. Pour over ham.
5. **Flash:** Let liquid get thick and sticky (2-3 minutes). Flip ham to coat.
6. **Eat:** Fast food for fast ape. 

**STAY STRONG:** Still rum. Still ham. Just fast.
