# 🦍 HOLIDAY RUM HAM RECIPE 🦍

## THE MEAT
- 1 Bone-in spiral sliced ham (8-10 lbs)
- 1 bottle dark rum (Cheap kind fine. Fire make it better.)

## THE GLAZE
- 1 cup brown sugar (Sand from trees)
- 1/2 cup honey (Bee spit)
- 1/4 cup Dijon mustard (Yellow mud)
- 1 tsp cinnamon (Brown dust)
- 1/2 tsp cloves (Little wood pins)
- Pineapples (Spiky fruit circles)
- Maraschino cherries (Red bird eyes)

## THE WORK
1. **Heat box:** Turn fire box to 325°F (165°C).
2. **Prep ham:** Put ham in deep metal tray. Flat side down. 
3. **Booze it:** Pour 1 cup rum over ham. Wrap tight in silver skin (foil).
4. **First fire:** Bake ham 10-12 minutes per pound.
5. **Glaze mix:** While ham gets hot, mix sugar, honey, mustard, spices, and 2 tbsp rum in bowl.
6. **Dress it:** 30 minutes before ham done, take out. Remove silver skin. Use little wood pins (toothpicks) to stick spiky fruit and red eyes to ham.
7. **Paint it:** Brush glaze all over ham. 
8. **Final fire:** Put back in box without silver skin. Turn fire to 400°F (200°C) for 15-20 minutes until sticky and black-brown.
9. **Serve:** Eat with hands. Drink more rum.

**MANDATORY:** Do not drop ham in ocean. Rum ham is precious.
